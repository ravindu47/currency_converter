🚀 Currency Converter - University Project

💡 The Problem

International students often struggle to convert currencies accurately and quickly. This tool simplifies that process.

🔧 The Solution

This CLI-based currency converter allows users to quickly get exchange rates for any currency.

🌍 Who Is This For?

Designed specifically for international students who need quick and accurate currency conversions.

📖 How to Use

cargo run 100 LKR EUR

Replace 100 with your amount, LKR with the source currency, and EUR with the target currency.

📊 Example Output

100 LKR is approximately 0.25 EUR

✨ Features

  • ✅ Convert between multiple currencies
  • ✅ Real-time exchange rates
  • ✅ Simple CLI-based interface
